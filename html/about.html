About Me

I'm Edele — a gentle wanderer, a keeper of quiet stories, and your guide through the Gibelverse.

I’ve never been drawn to maps with thick lines or blinking destinations. My path has always followed something softer, a shimmer on the horizon, a breeze that hums in a forgotten language, a feeling that something beautiful is waiting just a little further. I was raised on Sereth Prime, surrounded by floating libraries and drifting gardens, where silence wasn’t empty, it was sacred.

I don’t travel to collect places. I travel to remember them. My vessel, the Calm Current, is a quiet companion, slow, deliberate, full of creaky drawers and soft corners. I carry journals made of moss-paper, tiny keepsakes from markets lit by auroras, and field recorders that hum with the sound of alien lullabies. I stay long enough to let a city teach me its rhythm. Sometimes I leave a note behind, tucked into a rock or under a plate.

This blog is my starmap of wonder. The Gibel Universe isn’t just a place , it’s a feeling. Every planet, every story here, is something I experienced with open palms. I’ve eaten meals grown from empathy. I’ve danced with fog spirits. I’ve watched a comet cry pollen onto a sleeping lake. And I’ve written it all down, for me, and for you.

I believe travel isn’t about the loudest sights. It’s about the quiet things that change you. A glow that answers your sadness. A stranger who smiles like they’ve known your dreams. A forest that feeds you what you didn’t even know you were missing. I don’t choose where to go, the universe nudges me, and I follow.

So thank you for arriving. Whether you’re a fellow dreamer, a cautious traveler, or just someone searching for softness in a chaotic galaxy, you’re welcome here. I hope these pages give you what you didn’t know you needed. I hope something here stays with you.
